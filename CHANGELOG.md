## [v0.1.0] - 2026-03-07

### Highlights

- Auto-approve tool requests for AI coding CLIs (Kiro, Claude Code, Codex, Gemini) running in tmux
- Kiro multi-subagent support - automatically cycles through and approves all concurrent subagents
